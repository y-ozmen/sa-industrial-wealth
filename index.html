The 'Pracademic' Talent Gap

The scarcest asset is the "unicorn" archetype: PhD-level mechanical engineering rigor paired with authentic, lived mastery of Japanese manufacturing culture. Three tracks demand it — Tier 1 TPS leadership, SwRI applied research, and UTSA/CAMLS academic direction.
